Solstice Coffee

A full identity system for a third-wave roaster expanding from one cart to six retail locations.

The Brief

From a single cart to a six-location roaster

Solstice came to us with a loyal following and a brand that hadn't kept pace with their growth. Packaging was inconsistent across SKUs, the logo didn't scale to a storefront sign, and the team had no system for new product lines.

We rebuilt the identity around the actual experience of drinking Solstice coffee: bright, unhurried mornings. That became the throughline for the new mark, palette, and packaging system.

The Outcome

A system the in-house team now runs independently

Within four months of launch, Solstice opened two new locations using the new signage system without bringing in outside help — exactly the kind of self-sufficiency the guidelines were built for.
