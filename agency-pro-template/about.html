Our Story

Eleven years of building brands that hold up under pressure

Forge & Co opened in 2014 above a metal fabrication shop in Portland — which is where the name, and the obsession with craft, actually came from. We started doing identity work for two coffee roasters and a bike shop. We now work with growth-stage companies across the country, but the standard hasn't moved: every brand we touch should feel inevitable, not decorated.

We're not the agency that hands you a 90-slide deck and disappears. We stay in the work — strategy, design, build, and the campaign that follows — because brands fall apart in the handoffs between vendors. Keeping it under one roof is how we keep it consistent.
